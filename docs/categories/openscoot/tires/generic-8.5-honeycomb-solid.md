## Notes

Alternate-format entry for solid honeycomb (semi-solid) tires sold as
Xiaomi 8.5-inch family compatible. Honeycomb tires fit the same hub but
change the contact-patch behaviour entirely: no inner tube, no air
pressure, lower puncture risk, harsher ride. Listed separately rather
than as a variant of the pneumatic generic because the ride and wear
profile are not interchangeable. ETRTO size codes are designed for
pneumatic tires and do not cleanly describe a solid honeycomb; the
imperial 8.5-inch callout is what marketplaces use.
