## Notes

Anti-formaldehyde variant (purple-band) of the M8R cartridge. Same
housing as the original M8R-FLH so it drops into any Mi 3-family unit;
the difference is a heavier activated-carbon stage targeted at gaseous
pollutants. Xiaomi states the unit "removes formaldehyde to ASHRAE
standard" but does not publish a carbon weight figure on the global
store page. An antibacterial-media variant (M8R-FLG) also exists with
the same housing; not added as a separate entry until a verifiable
product page is found.
