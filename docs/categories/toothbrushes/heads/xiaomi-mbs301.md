## Notes

Officially listed by Xiaomi as compatible with Mi Electric Toothbrush, T300,
T500, and T500C. The cross-fit between MES602 (T300) and MES601 (T500) is
the project's evidence for grouping these handles under one mount family.
T700 (MES604) is excluded from this manufacturer head listing despite
marketplace sellers regularly bundling it; whether that bundling is
mechanically accurate is one of the Batch A questions.

A "sensitive" variant exists as a separate Xiaomi product page; its mount
is the same, only the bristle profile differs.
